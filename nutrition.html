Nutrition Plans

Mediterranian Diet Plan

The Mediterranean diet emphasizes whole, nutrient-dense foods, featuring a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, nuts, and healthy fats, particularly olive oil. It includes moderate consumption of fish and poultry, while limiting red meat and sweets. Known for its heart-healthy benefits, this diet promotes longevity and overall well-being, encouraging a balanced and sustainable lifestyle.

Keto Diet Plan

The Keto diet is a low-carbohydrate, high-fat eating plan designed to induce ketosis, a metabolic state where the body burns fat for fuel instead of carbohydrates. Typically comprising 70-75% fat, 20-25% protein, and 5-10% carbs, this diet aims for rapid weight loss and has been associated with various health benefits, including improved mental clarity and reduced hunger.

Atkins Diet Plan

The Atkins diet is a low-carb eating plan divided into four phases, starting with a strict limit on carbohydrate intake to kickstart weight loss. As participants progress, they gradually reintroduce carbs while focusing on high protein and healthy fats. This flexibility allows for a personalized approach to weight management, making it easier to maintain in the long term.
